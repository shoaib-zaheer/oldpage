About

Shoaib Zaheer an afghan artist, at present base in Belgium, I am self study & motivated artiest, able to work with different art media (water color, oil color, pencil, charcoal, calligraphy, miniature, wood Mosaic) computer graphic designer, animator, illustrator, 3D model, video editor amd HTML, CSS code writer, with little bit JavaScript knowledge.


My art is exploration and affirmation into the emotion of subjects and idea by amalgamate of colors, forms and textures, to find those sentiment in our surroundings.


Currently one of recognized artist, sponsor by `Kunstencentrum Vooruit vzw / Arts Centre Vooruit` ( Introduce to INFRA to inaugurate in project 2019- 2020- 2021 for refugee artists).
Participated in 2019 ODC (Open Design Course) `KASK School of Arts Gent` displayed short 7 animation series.
Migrations of art Art exhibition `Gallery 59 (Peruwelz)` along with Mr. Eddy Devolder and Mr. Aime M Pane.
Peace of Art (Brussels) along with 50 more Belgium artiest,
Petit Chateau solo art exhibition of water color `Tournai`.
Night school of Art one week art workshop and exhibition `Tournai`.
